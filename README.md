# Surf Share

A platform for sharing surf spots, conditions, and experiences with the surfing community.

## About

Surf Share is designed to help surfers discover new spots, share real-time surf conditions, and connect with fellow wave riders. Whether you're a seasoned surfer or just starting out, this platform provides the tools you need to make the most of your time in the water.

## Features

- **Spot Discovery**: Find surf spots near you or explore new locations
- **Condition Sharing**: Get real-time updates on wave conditions, wind, and tide
- **Community**: Connect with other surfers and share experiences
- **Safety**: Access important information about currents, hazards, and local rules

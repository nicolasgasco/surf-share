Welcome to the SurfShare API!

These are the endpoints that are currently supported:

  • Breaks:
    • GET /breaks: list all available surf breaks.
    • GET /breaks/{slug}: retrieve detailed information about a specific surf break identified by its slug.
  • Auth:
    • POST /auth/register {username, email, password}: register a new user.
  • Forecast:
    • GET /forecast/{slug}: retrieve marine and weather forecast data for a specific surf break identified by its slug.
